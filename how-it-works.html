How It Works

It's Just 4 Easy Steps

Step 1: Upload Your Resume

Start by uploading your resume and pressing Continue. Our AI will analyze your experience to personalize your outreach.

Step 2: Define Your Search

Enter who you're looking for and how many results you want. Click Find and let JarvisNetwork discover the best matches for you.

Step 3: Add Outreach Samples

Upload up to 3 of your previous outreach emails. The AI will learn your unique tone and style to craft messages that sound just like you.

Step 4: View Results & Send

Review your results—top matches are most likely to connect. When you're ready, press Export Emails to send your personalized messages.
